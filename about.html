Our Story

About AutoCare Garage

AutoCare Garage is a trusted service center focused on transparent pricing, reliable workmanship, and long-term vehicle health.

Mission

To deliver professional and affordable vehicle servicing with speed, integrity and precision.

Vision

To become the leading customer-first garage brand known for dependable quality service.

Values

Safety, honesty, teamwork, accountability and continuous technical improvement.

Our Growth Timeline

2015 - Garage Opened

Started operations with a small team focused on routine maintenance.

2018 - Expanded Workshop

Added more service bays and specialist tools for faster service delivery.

2021 - Computer Diagnostics Introduced

Integrated modern fault diagnosis technology for accurate troubleshooting.

2025 - 1000+ Happy Customers

Reached a major trust milestone with strong customer retention and referrals.
